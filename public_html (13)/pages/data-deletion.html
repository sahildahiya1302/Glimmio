Data Deletion Instructions

Your privacy is important to us. If you wish to request deletion of your personal data from our systems, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

  • Send an email to home@glimmio.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  • Include your full name, email address, and any relevant account information to help us identify your data.
  • Specify the data you want to be deleted or request complete deletion of all your personal data.

What Happens Next

Upon receiving your request, we will verify your identity and process the deletion within 30 days, unless otherwise required by law.

Important Notes

  • Data deletion requests may affect your ability to use certain services.
  • We may retain some information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
